## What This Site Should Show

The main goal is to show gameplay systems work clearly: what the system does,
why it was built that way, and how it supports iteration.

## Keeping The Focus Balanced

A lot of my experience touches tools and authoring workflows, but I do not want
the portfolio to read as tools-only. The stronger framing is gameplay systems
first, with designer-friendly workflows as part of how those systems are built.
